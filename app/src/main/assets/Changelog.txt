Version 1.0.0 - 2026-06-23
 * Rebuilt LoanPro as a modern Android project.
 * Improved entry, percent, backspace, clear, and invalid-result behavior.
 * Added adaptive launcher icons and themed icon support.
 * Removed legacy analytics, unused permissions, licensing checks, and paid/lite wrapper projects.

Version 1.2 - 08/15/2012
 * Fixed some issues related to decimals (calculation and display)
 * Added first phase of Bi-Weekly support (informational, see note below)

Note: Bi-Weekly information is available via Shift + Bi-Wkly (Term) button.  Information shown is the updated Term, Interest Savings, Total Interest, Total Principal and Total P+I.  Once in Bi-Weekly mode, continue pressing Term for further information.

Version 1.1 - 07/25/2012
 * Added support for Loan-to-Value (LTV)
 * Added support for Private Mortgage Insurance (PMI)
 * Fixed several issues related to setting and calculating price, loan amount and down payment.
 * Removed analytics package and unused permissions.

Note: LTV and PMI are accessible via the Shift button.
