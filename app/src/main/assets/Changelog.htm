LoanPro 1.0.0

2026-06-23

A modern open-source rebuild of the original LoanPro calculator.

  • Restored fast calculations for amount, payment, term, and interest.
  • Improved number entry, percent, decimal, backspace, clear, and repeated-use behavior.
  • Added useful invalid-result messages, including Pmt Low and Rate High.
  • Added adaptive launcher icons, themed icon support, and refreshed listing artwork.
  • Removed analytics, unused permissions, licensing checks, and legacy wrapper projects.

Privacy and Packaging

  • No ads, analytics, tracking, network permission, Play Services, Firebase, or crash-reporting SDKs.
  • App state stays on device in Android app preferences.
  • Release metadata, privacy notes, changelog, and Apache-2.0 license are included for store/F-Droid review.
