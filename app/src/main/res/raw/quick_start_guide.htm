Thank you for your purchase of LoanPro!

Before using LoanPro, please read over this quick start guide to learn how to use the easy yet powerful features.

Basic Usage
LoanPro has four main units of calculation; Loan Amount, Monthly Payment, Term and Interest. You can calculate any one of these values by entering the three remaining values and clicking the button for the value you wish to calculate. Read the following sections for examples of common calculations that you can perform with LoanPro.

Monthly Mortgage Payment (P+I)
To find the monthly Principal and Interest payment on a 30-year fixed-rate loan of $150,000 at a rate of 4.75% interest, perform the following steps:
  1. Hold the Clear button to clear all stored values.
  2. Enter 150000 and click the Amt button.
  3. Enter 30 and click the Term button.
  4. Enter 4.75 and click the Int button.
  5. Click the Pmt button to view your monthly payment of $782.47.

Loan Amount
Find out how much you can borrow if you can afford $150 per month and can get a 3-year loan at 7.9% interest:
  1. Hold the Clear button to clear all stored values.
  2. Enter 150 and click the Pmt button.
  3. Enter 3 and click the Term button.
  4. Enter 7.9 and click the Int button.
  5. Click the Amt button to view your loan amount of $4,793.82.

Number of Payments
Find out how long it will take to pay off your loan of $12,000 while making $200 payments at a 4.9% interest rate:
  1. Hold the Clear button to clear all stored values.
  2. Enter 12000 and click the Amt button.
  3. Enter 200 and click the Pmt button.
  4. Enter 4.9 and click the Int button.
  5. Click the Term button to view your term of 5.75 years.
  6. Click the Term button again to view your term of 68.97 months.

Interest Rate
To find the interest rate on a loan of $25,000 with a 5-year term and monthly payments of $500, perform the following steps:
  1. Hold the Clear button to clear all stored values.
  2. Enter 25000 and click the Amt button.
  3. Enter 500 and click the Pmt button.
  4. Enter 5 and click the Term button.
  5. Click the Int button to view your interest rate of 7.42.

Principal, Interest, Tax and Insurance Payment (PITI)
To find the monthly PITI payment on a 30-year fixed-rate loan of $375,000 at a rate of 5.25% interest including 1.25% property tax and $800/year home owners insurance, perform the following steps:
  1. Hold the Clear button to clear all stored values.
  2. Enter 375000 and click the Amt button.
  3. Enter 30 and click the Term button.
  4. Enter 5.25 and click the Int button.
  5. Enter 1.25 and click the Tax button.
  6. Click the Tax button again to view the annual tax is $4,687.50.
  7. Enter 800 and click the Ins button.
  8. Click the Pmt button to view your P+I payment of $2,070.76.
  9. Click the Pmt button again to view your PITI payment of $2,528.06.

Payment Based on Sales Price and Down Payment
To find the monthly payment on a 30-year fixed-rate purchase price of $275,000 at a rate of 4.5% interest including a down payment 20% down payment, perform the following steps:
  1. Hold the Clear button to clear all stored values.
  2. Enter 275000 and click the Price button.
  3. Enter 20 and click the Down button.
  4. Click the Down button again to view the down payment of $55,000.
  5. Click the Amt button to view the loan amount of $220,000.
  6. Enter 30 and click the Term button.
  7. Enter 4.5 and click the Int button.
  8. Click the Pmtbutton to view your monthly payment of $1,114.71.
